Vision towards creating digital twin of the building stock to reduce the environmental burden of built environment and enhance occupant's health, comfort & wellbeing.

ergEnomics is a Tech based solution which uses least sensors to digitize an existing building into a ‘smart building’ that can communicate the essential data to helps make informed decision about your facility towards energy efficiency and a healthier, comfortable & more productive indoor environment.

Our Solution

On an average a medium sized commercial establishment spends around INR 40-50 lacks on their electricity bills (average EUI is 150kWh/m2 and average built up area 7500m2). Our solution can help them optimize and save at least 20% of this annually. Moreover, after the COVID pandemic the uncertainty in operating buildings and the responsibility for buildings to provide a healthier and safer indoor working environment has increased tremendously. Therefore, optimizing building’s operations becomes an imperative to not only save money but ensure a healthy and safe working environment.
